# NZ Healthcare Access Current Guidance

As of: `2026-04-17`

This file is the baseline summary for healthcare-access questions in New Zealand. Eligibility rules, clinic enrolment, local service availability, and costs can change. Verify official health or provider sources before answering any current operational question.

## Official Sources

- Health New Zealand information site: https://info.health.nz/
- Government NZ health overview: https://www.govt.nz/browse/health/
- Immigration New Zealand healthcare setup guide: https://www.immigration.govt.nz/live/setting-up-your-life-in-new-zealand/getting-health-care-and-finding-a-doctor/
- Healthpoint provider directory: https://www.healthpoint.co.nz/
- Use the relevant university's official student health page for campus-specific questions.

## Common Use Cases

- Student feels sick and wants to know whether to go to student health, GP, urgent care, or pharmacy
- New arrival wants to understand how GP registration works
- Student wants to know what to do outside campus clinic hours
- User wants help finding local healthcare services after moving cities

## Stable Framing

- Newcomers often need system-navigation help more than medical explanations.
- The right answer depends on urgency, location, enrolment status, and provider availability.
- Current local service details and pricing should be verified before being presented as operational advice.
