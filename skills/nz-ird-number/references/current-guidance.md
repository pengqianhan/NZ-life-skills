## Baseline Use Cases

- New international student wants to work part-time and needs a tax number
- New arrival wants to know whether to open a bank account before applying
- Student received the number and now needs help with tax code or employer setup

## Stable Framing

- An IRD number is the tax identifier used for employment, interest income, and other tax interactions in New Zealand.
- Students commonly need it soon after arrival if they plan to work or interact with banking and tax systems.
- Exact application steps, evidence rules, and processing expectations should be treated as current-policy details.
