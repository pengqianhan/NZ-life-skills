# NZ Renting Basics Current Guidance

As of: `2026-04-17`

This file is the baseline summary for newcomer rental questions in New Zealand. Tenancy rights, bond handling rules, notice rules, and required documentation can change. Verify official tenancy guidance before answering any current legal or operational question.

## Official Sources

- Tenancy Services: https://www.tenancy.govt.nz/
- Ministry of Business, Innovation and Employment housing and tenancy context: https://www.mbie.govt.nz/
- Government NZ housing overview: https://www.govt.nz/browse/housing-and-property/
- Kāinga Ora for public-housing context only: https://kaingaora.govt.nz/

## Common Use Cases

- Student deciding between university accommodation, flatting, or a private rental
- Student asks what documents are usually requested in a rental application
- Student wants to understand bond, rent in advance, and move-in expectations
- Student needs help reading a tenancy agreement or condition report

## Stable Framing

- Housing questions often mix practical advice with legal rights.
- A good answer should separate common market practice from official tenancy rules.
- Current tenancy-law questions require live verification from official government guidance.
