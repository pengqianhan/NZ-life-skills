# NZ Renting Basics

## Overview

Use this skill to give a newcomer practical housing guidance while staying within current official tenancy boundaries. Renting advice can drift into legal territory, so verify live official tenancy guidance for current rules.

## Workflow

1. Determine whether the user needs:
   - temporary housing versus longer-term rental
   - flatting versus formal tenancy explanation
   - rental application document checklist
   - bond, rent-in-advance, or move-in questions
   - interpretation of a tenancy document
2. Read [references/current-guidance.md](references/current-guidance.md).
3. For any legal or rule-sensitive question, verify the current official tenancy source first.
4. Answer in plain language and separate:
   - everyday rental practice
   - current official tenancy rights and obligations

## Source Order

1. Official New Zealand tenancy guidance
2. Official bond and tenancy service pages
3. University accommodation pages for campus-specific housing context

## Answering Rules

- Explain the housing path clearly:
  - temporary accommodation
  - flatting with others
  - direct tenancy with landlord or property manager
- Cover practical student concerns:
  - documents commonly requested
  - viewing and screening
  - bond and rent timing
  - move-in condition checks
  - utilities and internet handoff
- Do not guess current legal rights or notice rules. Verify before answering.
