## Common User Questions

- Which bank is easiest for a new international student?
- Can I open an account before I have permanent housing?
- What counts as proof of address?
- What do I need before I start a part-time job?

## Stable Framing

- Most newcomers need a local bank account early for rent, wages, and day-to-day card use.
- The exact required documents vary by bank and can depend on immigration status, address evidence, and whether the account is opened online or in branch.
- Practical follow-up topics usually include debit card delivery, mobile banking setup, EFTPOS usage, and linking payroll.
