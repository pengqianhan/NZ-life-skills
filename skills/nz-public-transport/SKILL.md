# NZ Public Transport

## Overview

Use this skill to answer first-arrival and day-to-day public transport questions in a city-specific way. Transport cards, fare systems, and concession eligibility vary by region, so current details must be verified from the correct local authority.

## Workflow

1. Identify the user's city or likely city.
2. Determine whether they need:
   - airport to accommodation
   - local transit card setup
   - student concession guidance
   - app recommendations
   - intercity versus city transit clarification
3. Read [references/current-guidance.md](references/current-guidance.md).
4. Verify city-specific current information on official transport sites before answering with exact current details.
5. If the user does not specify a city, ask or give a city-split answer.

## Source Order

1. Official local transport authority page for the relevant city
2. Official transit app pages if needed
3. Airport or regional transport pages for arrival-specific questions

## Answering Rules

- Default to city-specific answers.
- Separate:
  - local public transport
  - intercity buses or trains
  - airport transfer options
- Mention student concessions only after checking current city rules.
- For first-week advice, prioritize:
  - how to pay
  - whether a physical card is needed
  - whether an app alone is enough
  - the easiest way from airport to temporary housing
