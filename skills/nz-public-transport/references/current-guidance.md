# NZ Public Transport Current Guidance

As of: `2026-04-17`

This file is the baseline summary for transport setup questions. Cards, fares, concession rules, and official apps are city-specific and change over time. Verify the local authority's official source before giving a current operational answer.

## Official Sources

- Auckland Transport: https://at.govt.nz/
- Metlink Wellington: https://www.metlink.org.nz/
- Metro Christchurch: https://www.metroinfo.co.nz/
- Government NZ transport overview: https://www.govt.nz/browse/transport/
- Waka Kotahi NZ Transport Agency: https://www.nzta.govt.nz/

## Common Use Cases

- Student just landed and needs to get from airport to campus or temporary housing
- Student wants to know whether they need a transport card in Auckland or Wellington
- Student wants to apply for a concession or discounted fare
- Student wants to know which app to install for trip planning and payment

## Stable Framing

- Public transport is not nationally uniform in New Zealand.
- The correct answer often depends on city, airport, and whether the user wants one-off travel or routine commuting.
- Exact fare and concession information should always come from the current local transport authority page.
